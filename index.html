Social Network Sharing Plugin

jsSocials is a simple social network sharing jQuery plugin. It's flexible and easily extensible. Configure visual appearance. Choose one of several themes provided. Add any yet unsupported social network if needed.

Download jsSocials  

jsSocials Provides

Tuning

jsSocials allows to easily customize appearance. Define how should look your share controls considering screen width.

Themes

jsSocials supports several themes. Chose the most appropriate for you design.

Custom Shares

jsSocials gives ability to register any custom share with ease.
